We Are A Charity Organization

Recent pandemic, corona virus, has badly effected families of daily wage.

Due to massive infection and rapid increase in corona infected patients, government has shutted down all forms of earnings except specified shops and job type where families living on daily wages are badly effected as they cannot earn anymore so far. This effort is to support those families who find it hard to get food two times a day.

This organiztion will work in a geographical limit of Sherdeen Banda, Thand koi. All residents of this area are helping us by either working with is or by donations.
